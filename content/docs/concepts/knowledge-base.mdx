## Overview

Knowledge Base is the context layer for your assistant. You upload your business content, and Amarsia prepares it so your assistant can answer with relevant details instead of guessing.

This reduces hallucinations and cuts repeated support work because answers stay grounded in your actual material.

## Supported document types

Amarsia currently supports:

- PDF
- DOCX
- TXT
- CSV
- XLSX
- PPTX

## How it works

1. Create a knowledge base.
2. Upload one or more files.
3. Amarsia processes files in the background and marks them ready.
4. Attach that knowledge base to an assistant.
5. Test and deploy with context-aware responses.

For visual-heavy files, processing includes OCR/vision-based extraction so important content is not missed.

## Why this matters

- You do not need to design your own document pipeline.
- Different file types are processed with suitable extraction methods.
- Your assistant gets context that is easier to maintain than hardcoded prompts.
- Teams spend less time manually feeding context into each request.
